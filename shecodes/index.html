What is HPV?

Human papillomavirus (HPV) is a viral infection that is passed on through skin-to-skin contact. There are over 100 types of HPV, and some can cause genital warts or cervical cancer.

Should I Get Vaccinated?

Getting vaccinated for HPV is important, as it can help protect against certain strains of the virus that are most likely to cause cancer. Talk to your doctor or healthcare provider to see if getting vaccinated is right for you.
